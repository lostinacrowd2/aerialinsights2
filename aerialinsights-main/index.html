RTK Corrections on Every Flight

Real-Time Kinematic (RTK) is a GPS correction technology that delivers centimeter-level positioning accuracy during flight. A fixed ground station — or a network-connected CORS station — continuously monitors satellite signals and transmits live differential corrections to the drone, geotagging every LiDAR point and image with precise real-world coordinates the moment it's captured. The result is survey-grade data without time-consuming post-processing or the need to set up dozens of ground control points across your site.

Survey-Grade DEM Accuracy 2–3 cm horizontal & 3–6 cm vertical accuracy — precise enough for contour mapping, cut/fill analysis, and BIM integration from a single flight
No Ground Control Points Required RTK corrections eliminate the need to place and survey GCPs across your site, saving field crews hours of labor per project
Same-Day Deliverables Data is geotagged and survey-ready the moment the flight ends — point clouds, orthophotos, and DEMs ready for review and upload the same day
Time & Cost Savings 100+ acres mapped in under 30 minutes. Accurate first-time data prevents costly rework mid-project — grading errors, foundation misalignment, and re-mobilization costs are eliminated before they start
